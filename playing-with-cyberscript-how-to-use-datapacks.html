Playing with CyberScript (How to use Datapacks)

Missions of Night City (BROKEN TIL NOTICE but you can check example with Dirty Missions datapack, it's the same behavior 😉) :

WHEN ENABLING ANY DATAPACK, whether it be in bunches or one at a time, ALWAYS GO TO CyberScript SETTINGS TO REFRESH DATAPACK CACHE.

Download Mission of Night City datapack before and enable it.

Keystone MONC
Go to any Fixer (look in map to find them),
​
and open your missions journal.
Missions will be in "Available Quests" at the bottom.
​
A list of missions will be displayed for that fixer's faction. Each fixer has the same mission types, but heavily varies on the location they send you. The more missions you do for a fixer, the more Affinity points will be gained.
​

Go to your world map after track the quest to track the mappin, tracking is not automatic (Game restriction)

Places of Night City :

Download the Datapack before ! (Places of Night City) and enable it (Make sure to Refresh Datapack Cache)

Keystone MONC
  • Go to V's PC in V's Apartement (the 1st one !!)
  • ​
  • Click on Net, then EzEstates
  • Click on CyberScript Estates Button
  • ​
  • Choose a category
  • ​
  • Click On Buy to buy it, it will be displayed on your map as a green home icon.
    ​
map

Housing :

Now that you have bought a Custom Place, you will want to decorate it.
Go to your place, then select Places of Night City Datapack through Cycle Custom Interact action. (see How to install page for information)
​
Choose the interact Open Buyed Item, you will see every item that you have bought and that are not already placed (the number above is the amount)
​
Click on the item, depending on the item type, you will have 2 ways to move it :
  • Directly, it will follow V and V sight, place it where you want and select the interact Set Item to fix it. You can also use interact Roll, pitch and Yaw to rotate it.
  • Indirectly, You will have to use Interact X+,X- , Y+/Y- and Z+/Z- to move it. You can also use interact Roll+-, pitch+- and Yaw+- to rotate it. These interacts can also be binded in CET for better use.
​
​

AV's of Night City

Download the Datapack before ! (AV of Night City) and enable it.

Keystone MONC
  • Go to Charter Hill in Westbrook
    ​
  • You will find an AV shop where there is a Vendor.
-Select AV's of Night City Datapack through Cycle Custom Interact action. (see How to install page for information)
​
​
  • Look at the Vendor and Click on Buy an AV interact
  • ​
  • A menu will open and you can buy a type of AV
  • ​
  • Call it from the Garage. (it can have weird name)
  • ​
-Select Get in AV interact when you look at the AV
map
-Select Get in AV interact when you look at the AV. You will be able to drive it
​
AV's have a collision detection system, so if you are unable to move forward or backward, it means that the system detects a hitbox of an element (building, road).
Go upper to avoid collision (Up direction is collision free)
Control : For QWERTY Keyboard : Space = Up/Pitch Up Q = Down/Pitch DOwn W = Forward S = Backward A = Left Yaw D = Right Yaw
For AZERTY Keyboard : Space = Up/Pitch Up A = Down/Pitch DOwn Z = Forward S = Backward Q = Left Yaw D = Right Yaw
For Gamepad : Left Stick UP: Forward
Left Stick Right/Left: turn
RT : Up/Pitch
LT : Down
You can also change the view of the cockpit by using cycle interact key and selecting the cockpit view.

TraumaTeam Membership

Download the Datapack before ! (Trauma Team Membership) and enable it.

Keystone MONC
  • Open your phone contactlist, call CS Trauma Team Membership Service
    ​
  • A menu will open and you can subscribe to a Membership
    ​

Life Of Night City

Download the Datapack before ! (Life Of Night City) and enable it.

In CyberScript, when you do some quests or events, your affinity with some NPC can grow.
You can check it in Shard Menu, under CyberScript Section
​
To Call them and Invite them somewhere, go to your phone and select them
​
a dialog will appear
​
When you want to invite them, just select the dialog option and go to any custom place.
​
The NPC will join you and you will be able to do interactions with it depending on your affinity.
​
-Select the Life of Night City Datapack through Cycle Custom Interact action to see what interacts are available. (see How to install page for information)
