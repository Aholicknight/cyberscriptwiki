🔃 Share your datapack to our community!

Zip your datapack folder in zip format.
Go on our discord: Cyberscript Discord​
And share it the channel Datapack Sharing Channel​
You can also share it to NexusMod Website or other Cyberpunk 2077 discord. Include Cyberscript as requirement !
​

💻 Available on Keystone (Official Cyberscript datapack) :

Go to our discord and PM donk or Eli for request it.
After a test and inspection of your datapack, we can publish it on keystone and github repository so everyone can access it.
If you request is denied, we will explain it why and if it need some edits, we will give you guideline for improve it.
